VitalDiary Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VitalDiary collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal and health-related information when you use our mobile application.

1. About VitalDiary

VitalDiary is a personal health tracking application designed to help users record and monitor daily health information such as blood pressure, heart rate, blood sugar, body temperature, oxygen saturation (SpO₂), weight, medications, and other wellness records.

2. Information We Collect

Information Purpose
Name Account identification
Email Address Authentication and account management
Health Records Store and display your health history
Device Information Security, diagnostics and performance improvements
Crash Logs Fix bugs and improve app stability
Advertising ID Display Google AdMob advertisements

3. Health Information

VitalDiary may store information that you voluntarily enter, including:

  • Blood Pressure
  • Heart Rate
  • Blood Sugar
  • Body Temperature
  • SpO₂
  • Weight
  • Medicine Records
  • Personal Notes
  • Other health measurements entered by you

This information is provided solely by you and is never collected automatically.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core app functionality.
  • Display and organize your health records.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.
  • Authenticate your account using Google Sign-In.
  • Maintain application security.
  • Generate reports and statistics.
  • Provide customer support.

5. Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in using Google, we receive basic profile information such as your name, email address, and profile picture. We do not access your Gmail, contacts, or other Google account data.

6. Advertising

The free version of VitalDiary may display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous device identifiers and usage information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Third-Party Services

VitalDiary may use the following services:

  • Google Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Sign-In

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information. While we take reasonable precautions to safeguard your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

9. Data Retention

Your information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide our services or until you delete your account or request removal of your data.

10. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited information only:

  • To provide services through trusted third-party providers.
  • To comply with applicable laws.
  • To protect our legal rights.

11. Children's Privacy

VitalDiary is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your stored data.
  • Request account deletion.
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns.

13. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with an updated effective date.
